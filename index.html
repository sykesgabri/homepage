Hi. I'm Gabriel Sykes.

The problems with this content were as follows:

  • No scripts were written for the videos. •

  • I had poor presentation and acting skills. •

  • I also had an annoying squeaker voice (Which I thankfully don't have anymore, it started subtly dropping somewhere around October 2017 and then dropped a significant amount between early 2018 and mid 2019). •

  • My videos had very poor audio quality. The microphones I used were cheap and never good, I knew nothing about how to make audio sound good and that resulted in a lot of horribly bad audio. •

  • Most of my videos had poor video quality. I recorded using a cheap 480p 15FPS webcam, a cheap windows phone that I had in the very early days of my channel. Even though quality and framerate improved as I got better phones over the years I barely ever used a tripod and never used a gimbal which resulted in inconsitent or shaky footage. •


I plan to make better content by investing in better gear (I actually did my research and know exactly what stuff I wanna buy), writing scripts and working on improving my presentation and acting skills.
